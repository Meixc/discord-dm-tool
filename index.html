Important: Bot Permissions

This tool requires specific permissions and intents to work correctly. The `403 Forbidden` error indicates that your bot is missing these.

Under **Bot** settings in the Discord Developer Portal, enable the **SERVER MEMBERS INTENT** and ensure your bot has the **Send Messages** permission.

You may need to re-add your bot to the server after enabling these intents.
