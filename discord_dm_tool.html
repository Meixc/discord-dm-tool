Discord Mass DM Tool

Sends a direct message to all users in a specific server.

⚠️ Important: Bot Permissions

This tool requires specific permissions and intents to work correctly. If you're seeing "Failed to fetch" errors, it's likely due to missing permissions.

  • Under "Bot" settings in the Discord Developer Portal, enable the SERVER MEMBERS INTENT.
  • Ensure your bot has the Send Messages permission.

⚠️ **Warning:** Do not share this token. It gives full control of your bot.
